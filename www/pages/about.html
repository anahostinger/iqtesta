About This
Welcome to This

This - is a free and open source HTML mobile framework to develop hybrid mobile apps or web apps with iOS or Android (Material) native look and feel. It is also an indispensable prototyping apps tool to show working app prototype as soon as possible in case you need to. This is created by Vladimir Kharlampidi (iDangero.us).

The main approach of the This is to give you an opportunity to create iOS and Android (Material) apps with HTML, CSS and JavaScript easily and clear. This is full of freedom. It doesn't limit your imagination or offer ways of any solutions somehow. This gives you freedom!

This is not compatible with all platforms. It is focused only on iOS and Android (Material) to bring the best experience and simplicity.

This is definitely for you if you decide to build iOS and Android hybrid app (Cordova or PhoneGap) or web app that looks like and feels as great native iOS or Android (Material) apps.
